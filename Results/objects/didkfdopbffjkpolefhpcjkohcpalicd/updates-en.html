AmazingHiring Extension 12.1

Searching for candidates social profiles by email has just become easier with our recent extension update. What’s new:

  • Welcome new functionality of finding candidate profiles simply by hovering over their email on any website, google spreadsheet, CRM system, mailing client or ATS.
  • Feel like switching off the extension openings when hovering over? You can do so in settings, but will still be able to search by email via selecting it and using “Search by email in AmazingHiring” function in the right-click menu.

Our extension also got a design remake. Now you can move the extension wherever you want to in the browser window. Here’s how it works:

  • The extension automatically turns into a widget with key details, when you hover over or click the email. You can see which email you actually searched.
  • Drag the widget to any part of the window and it will stay on that position.
